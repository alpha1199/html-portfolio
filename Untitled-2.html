How to Construct a House

Step 1: Planning

Determine the type of house you want to build, create a budget, and obtain necessary permits.

Step 2: Foundation

Excavate the site, pour the concrete foundation, and allow it to cure properly.

Step 3: Framing

Construct the structural framework of the house using wood or steel beams.

Step 4: Roofing

Install the roof trusses, sheathing, and roofing material to protect the house from weather elements.

Step 5: Exterior Finishes

Apply siding, windows, doors, and other exterior finishes to complete the look of the house.

Step 6: Interior Finishes

Finish the interior of the house with drywall, flooring, paint, and fixtures.
